My REVIEW of Terrifier 2

Tyler Silvia April 14th, 2026 ★★★★★

Terrifier 2 really impressed me. I expected a basic horror movie, but it ended up being way more creative and entertaining than I thought. The energy, the style, and the creepy moments all stuck with me. It felt like a movie made by people who genuinely love horror, and that made it a lot of fun to watch.

What I liked most was how confident the movie felt. It doesn’t try to copy other horror films—it just does its own thing, and that made it stand out to me. Even with all the intense moments, it had a style and personality that made the whole experience exciting rather than predictable. It’s the kind of movie I’m glad I gave a chance.
